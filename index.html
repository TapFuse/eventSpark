What is EventSpark?

Mobile apps are no longer a future-leaning technology to explore someday, but a vital tool that attendees seek in order to customise and enhance their event experience. The EventSpark app is the essential companion for both organisers and attendees. It facilitates direct engagement with the speakers and ensures that they are more approachable by listing their biographies, documents and pictures, as well as the direct links to their respective presentations.

Key features

Manage schedule

Give your attendees the autonomy to build and manage their personal schedule.

Networking

Multiple platforms for your attendees to connect, and get to know each other and exchange ideas.

Live polling

Direct engagement between key speakers and the audience, through live poling and the discussion boards.

Manage data

Easily manage large amounts of data that are required to support sessions (e.g. scientific abstracts)

Advanced note taking

Enabling user to take notes alongside the presentations and across the app that can be converted and stored as pdf files.

Branded multi-event app

Create revenue opportunities through enhanced sponsor and exhibitor profiles as well as banner advertising.

Real time feedback

In built feedback and further data provides useful live and digitalized information to track the success of your event.

Integrated website

Build it yourself modular website that is fully integrated with your app CMS.

Increased event lifecycle

With the app being accessible days before and after the conference.

Cross-platform application

EventSpark app is accessible on all commonly used modern platrofms
